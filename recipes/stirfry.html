Chinese stir fry, something I usually cook sometimes as a meal prep of the week.

Ingredients

  • Beef steak cut - 500g
  • 2 garlic glove
  • Sesame oil - 1 tsp
  • Cornflour - 2 tsp
  • Soy Sauce - 2 tsp
  • Spring Onion - a bunch
  • ginger - 10g
  • onion - 1
  • Capsicum - 1
  • Oyster Sauce - 1tsp
  • Black pepper - 1tsp
  • Chinese cooking rice wine - 4tsp
  • chicken stock - 1 teaspoon
  • Rice

Preparing the stiry fry

  1. Marinate the beef by by adding 2tsp of soy sauce, 1tsp sesame oil, 2tsp of cornflour.
  2. Bring out a chopping board and chop the spring onions(5-7cm pieces), onions, ginger (strip pieces) & capsium (2.5cm).
  3. Get a bowl and add 1tsp oyster sauce, 1tstp black pepper,4tsp chinese cooking wine & chicken stock.

Frying the stir fry

  1. Preheat the wok pan to a high heat.
  2. Add vegetable oil in the wok and stir it around
  3. Add the beef in first and stir it around til almost ready (around 1 minute) then take it out. You will use this again later.
  4. Add thegarlic & ginger first til you can smell the aroma, then add the rest of the vegetables (spring onions,onions & capsium).
  5. Then add in the sauce into it and stir the whole thing in
  6. And finally add the beef and stir the whole wok for another minute.

And thats how you make a simpe stiry fry meal.
