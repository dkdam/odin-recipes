Taco

Something I like to make once in a while.


Home

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Salsa
  • Minced beef 500g
  • Mexican seasoning
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Lettuce
  • 1 Lime
  • Diced can tomatoes
  • Shredded Cheese

The beef

  1. Preheat a pot and add oil
  2. Diced the onions in mini piece and cook them in a pot till brown (2 minutes)
  3. Add the ground beed til brown again then add in the mexican seasoning and stir it (5 minutes)
  4. Now add in the diced tomatoes and stir it for another 5 minutes. Or.. you could lower the heat of the pot place the lid on it and shimmer it for 40 minutes for maxium flavour.

The taco, The toppings

  1. Dice the tomatoes,lettuce & onions
  2. Preheat the oven to 180-200 degree celius and place the taco shells inside for a few minutes keep an eye on it.
  3. When the beef is ready, bring out the tacos and place the following order.
    1. Beef
    2. Onions
    3. Lettuce
    4. Tomatoes
    5. Salsa
    6. Shredded Cheese

Most important is to make sure you decorate it and serve nicely on the platter.
